Questions and Answers

What does verification of your property mean? ▸
Property verification simply means ensuring that each property is a legitimate business. Our team verifies all properties listed on Hostelworld, and all we ask of you is to provide us with the requested documents.
What is Hostelworld commission? ▸
Listing your property on Hostelworld is free of charge. However, we charge a commission (15% of the total booking price) for each booking made through Hostelworld.
How do guests pay? ▸
When guests book through Hostelworld, they pay a deposit that confirms their reservation at your property. This deposit is our commission. Customers then pay the remaining balance directly to you, choosing from your preferred payment methods. As our commission is received upfront there’s no need for any booking reconciliation, which keeps things simple.
Which channel managers connect to Hostelworld? ▸
Hostelworld connects to 200+ channel managers.
