Booking Information

Making a booking on Hostelworld is simple:

  1. Enter your destination, dates, and number of guests in the search bar
  2. Browse through the available hostels and select one that suits your needs
  3. Choose your room type and number of beds
  4. Fill in your personal details and payment information
  5. Confirm your booking

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your booking.

Yes, you need to create an account or log in to make a booking. This allows you to:

  • View and manage your bookings
  • Receive important updates about your stay
  • Connect with other travelers staying at the same hostel
  • Leave reviews after your stay

Creating an account is free and only takes a minute.

Once your booking is complete, you'll receive:

  • An immediate confirmation page on the website
  • A confirmation email with your booking details
  • Access to view your booking in the "My Bookings" section of your account

If you don't receive a confirmation email within 24 hours, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Payment & Pricing

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (on iOS devices)
  • Google Pay (on Android devices)

Payment options may vary depending on your location and the hostel's policies.

This depends on the hostel's payment policy:

  • Deposit only: Some hostels require only a deposit (typically 10-15% of the total) at the time of booking, with the remaining balance paid directly to the hostel upon arrival.
  • Full payment: Other hostels require full payment at the time of booking.

The payment details will be clearly displayed during the booking process before you confirm your reservation.

Cancellation Policy

To cancel your booking:

  1. Log in to your Hostelworld account
  2. Go to "My Bookings"
  3. Find the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Booking"
  5. Follow the instructions to complete the cancellation

You'll receive an email confirming your cancellation.

Refund policies vary by hostel and depend on:

  • The hostel's specific cancellation policy (Free, Flexible, or Strict)
  • How far in advance you cancel
  • Whether you paid a deposit or the full amount

The cancellation policy is always displayed on the hostel's page before you book and in your booking confirmation.
