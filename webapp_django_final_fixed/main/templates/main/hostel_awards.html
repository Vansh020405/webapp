How We Select Our Winners

The HOSCARS (Hostelworld Customer Annual Ratings) are the most prestigious awards in the budget accommodation industry, recognizing excellence across the globe.

Based on Real Reviews

Winners are determined based on over 1.2 million customer reviews submitted during the previous year.

Strict Criteria

Hostels must have received at least 500 reviews in the past 12 months to qualify.

Global Recognition

Awards are given across multiple categories and regions to celebrate diverse hostel experiences worldwide.
